隐私政策

本应用不收集任何用户数据。所有内容与记录仅保存在您的设备本地，无账号系统、无网络传输、无第三方分析或广告追踪。

相册权限仅在您主动选择保存图片时使用，且仅用于向相册添加图片，不读取相册内容。

Privacy Policy

This app does not collect any user data. All content is stored locally on your device. No account, no network transmission, no analytics, no tracking. Photo library permission is used only to save images when you choose to, and never to read your photos.
